## Important Notice

The only official study guide for the citizenship test is "Discover Canada: The Rights and Responsibilities of Citizenship" and is available from Citizenship and Immigration Canada at no cost. If you use any other material to prepare for the citizenship test, you do so at your own risk.

## Citizenship Test Requirements

As of October 11, 2017, applicants aged **18-54 years** must demonstrate adequate knowledge of Canada and citizenship responsibilities through a citizenship knowledge test in English or French.
